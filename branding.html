Branding

Branding always starts with Design Thinking. Mind Mapping and mood boards are the first steps to success.

Different variations are created before moving on to the digital format. The clearer your direction the faster the digital stage will go.

For this mermaid, the idea came from an instagram challenge for the month of May, called Mer-May. I started with painting the face, then added the body and finally added the fin to the lower half. After the main focus was complete, I added an underwater backdrop to give the image more depth. At this point the layered painting was brought into photoshop where I created the underwater scene using filters and effects. Again stages were done in multiple layers to keep the document organized and easily editable, always working non-destructively.
